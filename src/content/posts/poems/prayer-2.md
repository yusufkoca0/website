Lord, before you I kneel <br>
In a structure not so well <br>
As I never had time to learn <br>
How to upturn. <br>
On this day of calendar <br>
With open palms I surrender.

I ask thee to teach me <br>
How to change gently.

Like how my fingers <br>
Learned to unclench a fist <br>
To embracing hands, <br>
Finger by trembling finger.

Like how winter <br>
Learned to warm up <br>
To a lively spring, <br>
Flake by melting flake.

Be gentle, please. <br>
Crack the shell <br>
But not the one inside <br>
Who is shivering.

Touch not my meaning <br>
But rearrange my grammar <br>
So that I sound clear <br>
To those foreign ears.

Bestow me love, <br>
That feel not like a chore. <br>
Bestow me joy, <br>
That feel not like a wound. <br>
Bestow me laughter, <br>
That feel not like a guard. <br>
Bestow me rest, <br>
That feel not like surrender.

Lord, I do not know how to pray <br>
Accept my attempt of faith.

Make me feel heard.
